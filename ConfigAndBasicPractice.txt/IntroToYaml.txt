Ansible playbooks are written in yaml only!

WHAT IS Yaml?

JUST A KEY VALUE PAIR THING!

!!!!! There must be a space after the colon on all the key value pair !!!!!

How Array/List is represented?

"-" indicates the elements of the array!

Array1.yaml
Fruits:
    - Orange
    - Apple
    - Banana

Array2.yaml
Vegetables:
    - Carrot
    - Onion
    - Tomato

How Dictionary/Map is represented?

Dict1.yaml
Banana:
    Calories: 105
    Fat: 0.4 g
    Carbs: 27 g

Dict2.yaml
Grape:
    Calories: 62
    Fat: 0.4 g
    Carbs: 16 g

!!!!!! EQUAL NUMNBER OF BLANK SPACE IS MANDAROTY!!!!!

Spaces in yaml:

Take Dicvt2.yaml,
If the file looks like,

Grape:
    Calories: 62
     Fat: 0.4 g
     Carbs: 16 g

One space on before Fat and Carbs themn this will become the properties of "Calories"! Or it will thorows a syntax error as Calories is already having the value.
