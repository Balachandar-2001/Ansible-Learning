You are a Network Engineer you have to automate the config and management of the network devices.

Major Network collection providers:
        1. cisco
        2. juniper
        3. arista

    To install the collection,
        lnnapa101#> ansible-galaxy collection network.cisco

    You can use the collection later on your playbooks.

Ansible Collections are the package of roles, modules, plugins etc
It is self contained - easily accessible and sharable.
